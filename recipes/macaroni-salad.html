Macaroni Salad

Completed, refigorated bowl of macaroni salad.

The macoroni salad is an amazing recipe to get lots of compliments. This recipe is eady to prepare and make for friends and family for side dishes with pasta, celery, onions, bell pepper, carrots, and pimentos for an outstanding combination.

This astounding recipr can please many people with its very tangy dressing that everyone loves!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups uncooked elbor macaroni
  • 1 cup mayonaise
  • 2/3 cup white sugar, or to taste
  • 1/4 cup distilled white vinegar
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons prepared yellow mustard
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup grated carrot (Optional)
  • 2 tablesppons chopped pimento peppers (Optional)

Directions

  1. Bring all ingredients
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook macaroni pasta in the boiling water, stirring occassionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Rinse under cold water and drain.
  3. Mix mayonaise, sugar, vinegar, mustard, salt, pepper, and macaroni pasta together in a large bowl.
  4. Stir in celery, onion, green pepper, carrot, and pimentos.
  5. Refigerate salad for at least 4 hours before serving but preferably overnight.
