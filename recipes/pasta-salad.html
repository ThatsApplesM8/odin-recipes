Pasta Salad

Completed Pasta Salad made with pasta, veggies, and salad dressing.

The pasta salad is a unique salad dish made with fusil pasta, cherry tomatoes, provolone cheese, salami, bell peppers, olives, piomentos, and italian salad dressing.

The overall dish is quick, easy to prepare, cook, and serve amonmgst guests, making it a simple, yet effective course dish amongst all. The recipe is only 247 calories a serving, and contains relatively equal amounts of fat, carbs, and protein.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package fusilli (spiral) pasta
  • 3 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 pound provolone cheese, cubed
  • 1/2 pound salami, cubed
  • 1/4 pound sliced pepporoni, cut in half
  • 1 large green bell pepperm, cut into 1 inch pieces
  • (10 ounce) can black olives, drained
  • 1 (4 ounce) jar pimentos, drained
  • 1 (8 ounce) bottle Italian salad dressing

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook fusilli pasta in the boiling water, stirring ocasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes. Drain.
  3. Combine pasta with tomatoes, cheese, salami, pepporoni, green pepper, olives, and pimentos in a large bowl. Pour in salad dressing; toss to coat.
  4. Enjoy!
